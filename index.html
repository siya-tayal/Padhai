One topic, five doors in.

Some students remember through repetition, some through pictures, some by filling in the gaps themselves. Spade doesn't force one style — it lets a student pick.

Memorize Mode

A Leitner-style flashcard queue — cards you fumble come back soon, cards you've mastered fade out slowly, never all at once.

Fill in the blanks

Text-completion drills for definitions and processes, so recall is active, not just recognition.

Animated diagrams

Step-by-step visual breakdowns for the diagrams your textbook only shows as one static picture.

Formula sheets & checklists

Every chapter's formulas on one page, plus a printable syllabus checklist to track what's left.

Syllabus coverage

Built chapter-by-chapter, honestly.
